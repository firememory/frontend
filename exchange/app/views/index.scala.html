Complete Openness & Transparency

Coinport Exchange is a cryptocurrency exchange that features full openness and transparency one can ever dream of. Users can verify our full-reserve claim based on our open data and source code. For the first time, a cryptocurrency exchange was done right!

WARNING: We are testing this exchange publicly with selected users. Anyone can sign up and test with us, but we do NOT guarantee your money back during this period. So play with small amount of money!

Open-data offers 100% transparency

We open all non-user private data, including

  • Deposit and withdrawal events
  • Order submission and cancellation events
  • All transactions
  • Periodical system states(snapshots)

All data are downloadable as JSON files. Event data can also be browsed online.

Open-sourced

Our matching engine is opensourced on github. Feel free to checkout our public repository to verify our logics. Our matching engine features:

  • A uni-direction take-maker model.
  • Event-sourced architecture.
  • Orders are matched immediately after submission - no one can intercept your action, this is guaranteed.
  • High thoughput.

Provable Full-Reserve

We provide timely proof of full-reserve of user assets. We never invest your money for our own profit. To make this possible, we provide to our users:

  • All of our code and hot wallet addresses.
  • Each and every account's balance in all currencies.

Bank Level Security

We take user privacy and security very seriously. The following measures are taken to achieve the highest level of security:

  • All traffic are SSL encrypted.
  • All event and snapshot data are encrypted using AES algorithm before saving to disk. Even our employees cannot access your private data without authorization.
  • We use cloud storage to backup all data.
  • Your password are hashed on client-side then encrypted on server side using Bcrypt.
