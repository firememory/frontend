Our Full Openness Policies bring you exceptional transparency.

Coinport Exchange is the world's first crytocurrency exchange that features complete transparency by implementing the Full Openness Policies: open-sourced, open-data, and provable 100% reserve.

Open-Sourced

Our matching engine will be opensourced on github. Visit our public repository to verify our matching code, and feel free to clone it. Coinport Exchange allows clients to have insight to all the internals.

Open-Data

All data (except user privacy) are open for browsing and downloading. All orders, transactions, deposits, withdrawals, balances, addresses of our hot/cold wallets are accessible. Our clients see all the details as well as the big picture.

Provable 100% Reserve

We keep all your asset uninvested. Still wishing for a Merkle-tree based proof of reserve? We give you more - every 30 minutes we offer our clients all new events and system snapshots. You can verify our reserve claims yourself using simple maths.

Privacy and Security

At Coinport, we take user privacy and data security very seriously; and we believe privacy and security can co-exist beautifully.

User Privacy
All data that can be used to identify a user in the real world are encrypted and well protected. This ensures the anonymity of our open-data, similiar to cryptocurrency public ledgers.
Security
All data are encrypted using AES before being persisted; passwords are hashed in the browser first then hashed again on server using Bcrypt algorithm. We keep multiple copy of all data and perform regular backups.
